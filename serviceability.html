OPERATION GUIDE:

Step 1: Inspecting the Machine

Examine the machine to ensure that all screws are intact there are no damages and no other issues that could impact its performance. This step is essential to ensure the operation of the labeling process.

Step 2: Applying Stickers

Affix a sticker, onto the machine as part of the preparations. This sticker can serve as an indicator or label for the machine itself aiding in organization and easy identification.

Step 3: Positioning Bottles and Adjusting Parameters

Align the bottle, within the machine ensuring it is positioned accurately for labeling. Also adjust parameters on the machine to tune the labeling process according to specific bottle dimensions.

Step 4: Power Up and Start

Turn on the power supply to activate the machine. Initiate the labeling process by pressing the start button on the control panel, beginning the sequence that applies labels to the bottles.

Step 5: Trial Run and Adjustment

Perform a trial run of the labeling process to test the machine's performance. Make necessary adjustments based on the trial findings, ensuring that the labels are applied accurately and efficiently.

Step 6: Sample Inspection and Adjustment

Inspect a sample of labeled glass bottles to identify any errors or issues. If any discrepancies are found, make the necessary adjustments to improve the labeling accuracy.

Step 7: Commence Labeling Process

After confirming the satisfactory outcomes from Steps 1 to 6, proceed with the actual labeling process. This step marks the official start of applying labels to the glass bottles.

Step 8: Machine Shutdown

Once the labeling task is complete, turn off the labeling machine using the control panel. This step ensures the safe and proper shutdown of the equipment, completing the labeling process

In case of Malfunction/ Accident:

Step 1: Push Emergency Push button

 Emergency push buttons provide an important safety purpose by offering a rapid and reliable way to stop machinery or operations in an emergency.
